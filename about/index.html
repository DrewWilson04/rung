About Rung Boards

My name is Andrew, I am a 17 year old from Ohio. I love just about any board sport there is. There are some pretty cool boards out there, however, there aren't many that are handmade and customizable, that is why I started Rung Boards. I chose the name Rung Boards because the definition of rung is "A crosspiece between the legs". I thought that fit perfectly for boarding sports. I would love for you guys to check out the board designs I already have, or reach out and we can potentially work something out! Email us or DM us on Instagram. We are currently perfecting our first board which will be a longboard and we have plans to release skateboards, skimboards, and snowboards in the near future! Stick around for when we release those!
